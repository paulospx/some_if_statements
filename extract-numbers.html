How it works

The extractor scans left-to-right and pulls every numeric token — integers, decimals, comma-separated thousands, currency amounts, percentages, and scientific notation. Results appear instantly as you type.

Recognized

  • 42 · 3.14 · -7 · +0.5 · .75
  • 1,234.56 · $4,512,000 · 23.5%
  • 1.2e10 · 3E-4

Typically skipped

  • Phone-style groups (555-1234 → 555, 1234)
  • Version strings (v2.1.3 → 2.1, 3)
  • Hex colors (#A1B2C3) and bare IDs

All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
